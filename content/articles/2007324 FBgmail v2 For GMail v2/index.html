--- title: FBgmail v2 For GMail v2 date: 2007-11-20 16:40:51 Z tags: ["Tech","Google","Gmail","Greasemonkey"] wordpress_path: /2007/11/fbgmail-v2-for-gmail-v2 --- Most everyone knows by now that Google released a major update for GMail some time ago. This broke most Greasemonkey scripts for GMail including my FBgmail v1. Google however, in their brilliance, released an "API" specifically for Greasemonkey on the new GMail! I have harnessed this to create a version of FBgmail for the new GMail.
