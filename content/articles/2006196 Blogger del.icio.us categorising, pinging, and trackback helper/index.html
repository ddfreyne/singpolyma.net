On the post-create page there is now a 'Trackback' textarea, instead of a form on the 'post complete' page. Enter one or more trackback URLs (each on it's own line in the textarea) to use the trackack feature. After publishing the post your trackbacks will be sent out automatically and the status will be displayed on the 'post complete' page. For more information on trackback, go to Wikipedia. There are also a couple minor new features for the categorising in this release. One is the option to use the first 200 characters of the post body in the extended field on del.icio.us, instead of the datestamp. The 'Tags:' label that one clicks to change settings is now blue, and your cursor should change into a hand when hovering it.
