--- title: Microformats Proxy date: 2007-02-14 14:57:00 Z tags: ["XOXOBlog","Microformats","Tools"] wordpress_path: /actionstream/microformats-proxy --- Tired of working with pages that do not have Microformats support yet? The Microformats Proxy is the tool for the job. Individual or globbed URLs can be set up via XPath to extract data and format it as microformats. In this first version only hCards are supported. All existing hCards and XOXO data are also maintained.
