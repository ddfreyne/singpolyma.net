--- title: Google Base Live date: 2005-11-16 15:12:00 Z tags: ["Tech","Websites","Google"] wordpress_path: /actionstream/google-base-live --- Google Base launched today. It seems to be a service that will host just about any content, from recepies to schedules to opensource projects. All items can be tagged and an arbitrary number of arbitrary fields with arbitrary datatype are available. This seems to be a service that aims at providing tools, not purpose, and you can do just about anything with it! An interesting idea, it'll be interesting to see where it leads.
